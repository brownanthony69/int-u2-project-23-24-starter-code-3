The School to Prison Pipeline

How it Starts

The school-to-prison pipeline is a disturbing societal phenomenon where the policies and practices within the education system contribute to the increased likelihood of students, particularly those from marginalized communities, becoming involved in the criminal justice system.

Who it Affects

The school-to-prison pipeline affects students of color, those with disabilities, and those from marginalized communities. It can lead to a lack of access to education, a lack of opportunities for learning and others.
